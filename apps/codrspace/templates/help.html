Comments

The comments on codrspace are managed by Disqus. You must register an account on Disqus and provide codrspace with a shortname. Then you must put the shortname in your settings

 

What is a shortname?

Please see what is a shortname on Disqus

What do I put in the Website URL field in Disqus?

Please use {{ SITE_URL }}/{{ user.username }}/

How do I secure my comments to just codrspace.com?

Go to http://yourshortname.disqus.com/admin/settings/advanced and under the trusted domains section put codrspace.com
